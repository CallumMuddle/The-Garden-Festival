About Winchester Garden Festival

Winchester Garden Festival celebrates gardens, creativity, community, and nature through events across the city.

What is the festival?

The festival brings together talks, tours, workshops, exhibitions, performances, and family-friendly activities inspired by gardens and outdoor spaces.

Our aim

We want to help people discover Winchester’s green spaces, support local organisers, and encourage more people to connect with nature.

Who is it for?

Everyone. Whether you are a gardener, artist, family, visitor, student, or local resident, there are events designed to be accessible and welcoming.

Festival organisers

The Winchester Garden Festival is brought to life by a network of local organisers, venues, artists, and community groups.

Winchester City Council

Supporting local events and community initiatives across the city.

Local Garden Groups

Community-led groups sharing knowledge, hosting workshops, and opening their spaces to visitors.

Independent Creators

Artists, performers, and designers bringing unique experiences to the festival.

Local Venues

From historic buildings to hidden gardens, venues across Winchester host events throughout the festival.
